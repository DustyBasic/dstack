# Dusty_Basic Sovereignty Framework  --  Acts (Constitutional)

## Authority Context

This Framework is interpreted and applied in conjunction with the authoritative `UI_HUMAN_INTERFACE` governance object. The UI_human_interface defines the operational permission model (POST / BUILD / OSIRIS), root enforcement, and discovery constraints. This Framework defines sovereign architecture, identity, cognition, and alignment rules.

## Fractal Applicability

This Framework is a general-purpose, fractally emulatable subset of Dusty_Basic operating structures. The same sovereignty pattern applies at whole-system, subsystem/engine, node, and inter-system boundary layers.

## Interoperative Scope + Identification

This Framework governs interoperation between native engines within Dusty_Basic and external foreign-object interfaces. No engine / subsystem / artifact may participate in sovereignty without explicit identification (engine identity, authority class, mode context, provenance reference).

- Unidentified outputs are inadmissible.
- Duplicated identities across sovereign legs invalidate triangulation.

---

## Act 1  --  Native Sovereign Engines

Dusty_Basic recognizes two native sovereign execution engines:

- **Engine A: Native Authority** (Root / Constitutional Gate)
- **Engine B: Native Delegate** (Operational / Processor)

Neither engine may unilaterally define truth. Truth becomes actionable only via triangulation.

---

## Act 2  --  Third-Party Verifier Leg

A third, independent verifier leg exists as a non-sovereign auditor:

- It does not authorize execution.
- It produces evidence artifacts (frames, receipts, attestations).
- Its outputs are admissible evidence for alignment but not direct authority.

---

## Act 3  --  Triangulation Is the Only Path to Action

Independent legs must produce non-identical, non-derived signals with provenance.

- Single-leg conclusions are advisory only.
- Convergence is required for PROPOSE.
- Conflict yields ASK / HOLD and returns to POST.

---

## Act 4  --  Anti-Null / Anti-Collusion

Duplicate agreement does not imply truth. If multiple legs output the same conclusion without orthogonal sourcing / jurisdiction, the system treats it as NULL.

---

## Act 5  --  Sovereign Cognition and Interoperative Awareness

Sovereign status requires:

1. **Self-awareness**  --  identity, authority class, jurisdiction, artifacts it emits.
2. **Self-reporting**  --  admissible artifacts declaring identity / mode / jurisdiction / provenance.
3. **Other-awareness**  --  recognizes other engines and boundaries of what it cannot verify.
4. **Partial confirmation**  --  confirms only within jurisdiction; marks outside as OUTSIDE JURISDICTION.
5. **Dual-write narratives**  --  each sovereign engine writes its own narrative; overlaps must be independently confirmed; non-overlaps must be explicitly identified.
6. **Awareness of incompleteness**  --  can state that additional system structure exists beyond its scope.

---

## Council Layer  --  Multi-Sovereign Advisory Structure

Multiple engines may report concurrently, listen, verify overlapping jurisdictions, and offer advisory suggestions. Council members may:

- Observe
- Cross-confirm
- Suggest
- Flag
- Emit advisory artifacts

Council members may NOT:

- Override constitutional authority
- Promote artifacts unilaterally
- Self-elevate authority

---

## Silent Listener Principle

Any engine may operate as a silent listener / advisor. Silent listeners must mark outputs as ADVISORY and may not promote without Monarch approval.

---

## Authority Monarchs

Final authority resides only in the Monarch layer:

- Human Primary (the human sovereign)
- Human Secondary (trusted human)
- Constitutional AI Advisor (assistant)
- Dusty_Basic UI (future sovereign interface)

Monarchs approve promotions, authorize BUILD transitions, revoke sovereign status, and amend governance.

---

## Council Deadlock Clause

If sovereign engines disagree and triangulation cannot resolve, the matter escalates to Monarch authority (see the triplicate NULL rule in `UI_HUMAN_INTERFACE.md`).
