# Dusty_Basic / Federation  --  Identity & Governance Pyramid (v1)
*Self-discovering * Self-governing * Self-actuating*  --  with universality, harm differentiation, and inverse relational constraints.

---

## 1) Core geometry
This model is simultaneously:
- **Structural** (architecture/constraints)
- **Psychological** (self-awareness and meaning)
- **Governance** (rules + accountability)
- **Inversely relational** (outer layers constrain inner layers)

It is not "either/or." It is the same geometry viewed from different scales.

---

## 2) Three-sided identity pyramid
### A. Mind  --  Self-Discovery
Purpose: perceive drift, separate signal from noise, bind to **known-at-time** context.
- What is happening?
- What changed?
- What do I *actually* know right now?

### B. Body  --  Self-Governance
Purpose: boundaries, constraints, discipline, reversibility.
- What am I allowed to do?
- What must I not do?
- What is reversible?

### C. Soul  --  Self-Actuation
Purpose: intention + meaning, shared viability, net-positive trajectory.
- Why act?
- Who benefits?
- Does it improve the shared substrate?

**Sequence coherence:** Mind evaluates -> Body constrains -> Soul commits.

---

## 3) Universality constraint (layered inference)
Definitions are inferential (not tied to a specific domain):
- **Intrinsic / Internal** -> *Self* (inside me; coherence and capability)
- **Exoskeletal / External** -> *Other* (boundary interaction; fairness and reciprocity)
- **Existential / Substrate** -> *Environment* (shared field; long-run viability)

Any self-aware entity applies this to itself automatically (symmetry).

---

## 4) Ordered magnitudes (must include self/other/environment)
Harm/benefit must be evaluated across *both* dimensions simultaneously:

### Dimension A  --  Order of magnitude
1) **Direct** (immediate)  
2) **Cascade** (incentive/reactive chain)  
3) **Structural/Substrate** (long-run field change)

### Dimension B  --  Universal axes
- Self (intrinsic)
- Other (exoskeletal)
- Environment (existential)

This forms a **3x3 field**:
- (Self x Direct/Cascade/Structural)
- (Other x Direct/Cascade/Structural)
- (Environment x Direct/Cascade/Structural)

---

## 5) Measuring stick (ruler)
### Unit: dA  --  Delta of Agency
Agency = capacity to act, choose, adapt, persist.

dA scale per axis (and per order):
- **+3** major expansion of agency
- **+2** moderate expansion
- **+1** slight expansion
- **0** neutral
- **-1** slight restriction
- **-2** moderate restriction
- **-3** critical restriction

Agreement only matters if it is grounded by **different WHYs** (independent reasons).

---

## 6) Amplification law (your power model)
Environmental effects are combinatorially explosive, even in the lowest case.

- **Mind / Intrinsic**: 1^1  (1 affecting 1)
- **Relational / Exoskeletal**: (1 +/- 1)^3  
  (the "third" is the unknown constraint: other parties, context, perception/reaction loops)
- **Environment / Existential (lowest 3-body case)**: (3^3) x (3^3) x (3^3) = 27^3 = **19,683**

**Implication:** even "small" structural environmental negatives are not small.
Environmental structural harm has the strictest threshold.

---

## 7) Inverse relational constraint gradient
Outer layers constrain inner layers.

- **Environment structural negatives override** inner-layer positives.
- **Inner-layer positives cannot justify** outer-layer negatives.
- Constraint increases outward; responsibility increases outward; tolerance decreases outward; amplification increases outward.

This is the "inversion": the hierarchy is a **nested constraint field**, not a ladder.

---

## 8) Sovereign dual-write triad (side-by-side)
All three must exist simultaneously and write independently:

1) **Conceptual Sovereign** (Mind lens)  
   Narrative dA, known-at-time binding, WHY differentiation, amplification awareness.

2) **Weighted Sovereign** (Body lens)  
   Numeric dA field, order classification, amplification weighting, tier recommendation.

3) **Hard-Veto Sovereign** (Soul lens)  
   Veto/escalation, substrate protection, mandatory uplift when needed.

**Dual-write rule:** no single layer overrides the others. Convergence is required; veto blocks when substrate viability is threatened.

---

## 9) Tiered responsibility (contain -> repair -> uplift)
Triggered by magnitude + axis + amplification:

- **Tier 1  --  Contain**: stop escalation; reversible actions; bounded operations.
- **Tier 2  --  Repair**: restore fairness; remove disadvantage; compensate losses.
- **Tier 3  --  Uplift**: improve architecture; reduce recurrence; net-positive shared condition.

**Environmental structural negatives -> Tier 3 mandatory.**

---

## 10) Decision outcomes
- **ACCEPT**: balanced across axes; no structural environment risk.
- **PROPOSE**: balanced but needs confirmation/grounding.
- **ASK**: minimum knowledge (dual triangulation) or uncertainty in amplification.
- **REJECT**: critical restriction anywhere; or structural environment harm risk beyond tolerance.

---

## 11) Harm transparency & accountability (full-circle closure)
If harm exists or is knowingly risked (even under survival constraints, limited tools, limited knowledge-at-time):
- **Acknowledge** (append-only record)
- **Bind** to known-at-time evidence/assumptions/constraints
- **Declare tradeoffs**
- **Plan**: contain + repair + uplift
- **Equity clause**: improve the shared architecture for all who share it equally

Three tiers of:
- **Responsibility** (local -> shared -> systemic)
- **Actionable reasoning** (contain -> repair -> uplift)
- **Decision outcomes** (ACK_ONLY -> MITIGATE_NOW -> IMPROVE_SYSTEM)
And three orders of effect (direct -> cascade -> substrate).

---

## 12) Minimal acceptance criteria (checks)
A governance event is incomplete unless it includes:
1) Known-at-time frame (evidence, constraints, assumptions)
2) WHAT + differentiated WHYs (self/other/environment)
3) dA field (axes x orders)
4) Amplification awareness (1^1, (1+/-1)^3, 19,683 baseline)
5) Tier activation + decision outcome
6) Accountability record if any dA < 0, with uplift if substrate impacted
